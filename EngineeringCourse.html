Engineering Courses

Before Engineering School :

I went to school in Limoges, it was at the Léonard Limosin Highschool that I obtained my general Baccalauréat with distinctions and European Section English mention. During this training, I specialized in mathematics, Physics and Chemistry.

After that, I followed the Classes Préparatoires aux Grandes Ecoles training course at the Bellevue Highschool, in Toulouse in the Physics-Chemistry Engineering Sciences (PCSI) streams in the first year and Physics Engineering Sciences (PSI) in the second year.

This training prepared me to take the Grandes Ecoles d'Ingénieur competitions via wich I was able to join my current establishment,Toulouse INP-ENSEEIHT.

During the Engineering School:

Today, I am at the Ecole Nationale Supérieure d'Electrotechnique, d'Electronique, d'Informatique, d'Hydraulique et des Télécommunications (ENSEEIHT), an engineering school part of the group Toulouse Institut Nationale Polytechnique (INP). I am studying in the Numeric Sciences Department learning Computer Sciences, Applied Mathematics, Network and Telecommunication and preparing for the ENSEEIHT Computer Science & Telecommunication Engineering diploma.

I participate in particular in courses such as Imperative Programming, wher we learn the algorithmic language, modeling where we learn the logic of Reasoning or even Optimization where we study the optimization issues. In parallel with this, I participate in the computer sciences association of the ENSEEIHT called net7 in order to develop my skills in development, programming and cyber-security.
